There can be various reasons for which there is a need to adjust the invoice value or tax. For instance, **deficiency in quality of goods or services supplied leading to partial or full reimbursement of the invoice value.** 

The provision for this in CGST is to issue a credit note to the recipient. Upon the issuance of the note in accordance with Section 34 of the CGST the tax liability of the supplier stands reduced.

The output tax liability is not reduced if the credit note is not made as per the time prescribed in the statute, which till 13th November of that year, and later disclosed in the tax return for the period in question. It also requires **matching** with the corresponding **reversal of ITC by the recipient** in his valid return for the same tax period or later
