# Indirect Transfer of Shares Dossier

## Exp. 5

- An asset being a share or interest in a company or an entity that is registered outside of India. 
- Shall be deemed to be, and shall always have deemed to be situated in India
- If it derives directly or indirectly its value from the assets located in India

Any share or interest in a company, is deemed to be situated in India if it derives its value substantially from assets located in India.
