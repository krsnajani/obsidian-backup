It involves the transfer of the whole of the business concern as a going concern (as it is).  Section 2(42C) of the IT Act, 1961 defines it in the following:

 > Transfer of one or more undertaking by any means for a lump sum consideration without values being assigned to the individual assets and liabilities in such transfer
 
It must thus constitute the following test
1. Business is sold off and as a going concern
2. It is for a lump sum
3. Materials do not indicate an item wise value of the assets transferred.
