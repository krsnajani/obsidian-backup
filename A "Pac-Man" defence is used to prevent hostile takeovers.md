A hostile takeover is one where the a target company is acquired without the consent of its board of directors

One of the tactics available to prevent hostile takeover is the use of a "Pac Man Defence" whereby a target company can turn the tables on the acquirer and instead takeover that would-be acquirer. The name refers to Pac-Man, a video game in which the protagonist is at first chased around a maze of dots by four ghosts. However, after eating a "Power Pellet" dot, he is able to chase and devour the ghosts.

For instance when Porche acquired 31.50% in Volkswagen increasing its stake to 74%.
