## Issue
The question is whether the Settlement Commission, coonstitututed under 245B of the Act has jurisdiction to reduce ot waive te interest chargeable under Section 234A, 234B, 234C of the Act while passing orders of settlement under Section 234D of the Act

## Submissions
### Department 
- It was argued that the constitution of the Commission is based on the concept of comprose and settlement and thus it has the power to waive or reduce the interest, even if statutorily mandated in view of the wording of Section 245D.
- Further, the Commission being an income tax authority under Section 245D the Commission being an income tax authority has all the powers of the Board which are incidental to the functions of the Commission, which includes the Board's powers under Section 119 of the Atct, to relax the rigour of Section 234A, B and C.  

The power to levy interest under ss. 6 is assumed by the Commission to be a substantive provision. The Court disagreed with this proposition. The substantive provision is ss. 4 which is concerned with the power to pass order with respect to such matters with which it is cncerned.
