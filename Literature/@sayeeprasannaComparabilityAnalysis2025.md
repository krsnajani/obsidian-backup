## Comparability Analysis

It includes the identification of similarities in the economically significant characteristics of related party transactions and distinguishing the role of the parties involved in the tested transactions.

The basics of CA involve a reading of the

- Functions performed
- Assets used
- Risks assumed

The steps taken in applying the comparability analysis form part of the TP Documentation

## Five Factors

### Contractual Terms

Information in the intra-group contracts, aid comparability. It is more prominent in certain TP methods that involve a strict comparison of a legal terms to determine ALP. 

### Functional Analysis

The idea is that compensation should be commensurate to the FAR by the parties in the transaction. Thus the function performed by the parties determine which comparable should be taken. A contractual term, does not determine the _actual_ function that the parties may undertake in the transaction.
