### Facts
- Canon India is a wholly owned subsidiary of Canon Singapore. The TPO completed the TP Reports and found that transactions except AMP was held to be at ALP. The TPO opined that the expenduture on AMP was attributable to the brand building activity for the principal, whereby corresponding benefit was passed on to the principal which was to be factored into the ALP.
- Though it was objected that a host of routing sales expenditure which was not in the nature of an advertisement was included in the scope of AMP expenditure. Thus the quantification of the AMP expenses was challenged.
