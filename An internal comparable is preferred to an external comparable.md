In comparability analysis, an internal comparable is a transaction between an unrelated party and one of the parties to a controlled transaction. They are generally considered to be more preferable to external comparable due to the following advantages.

1. Direct and closer relationship with the transaction, similar characteristics for the transaction and similar strategy
2. Easier availability of financial information. 

This has also been opined and accepted in [[@pramodkumarTecnimontICBLtd.md]]

But for this too, there have to be relevant adjustments that are made to the comparable selected, since the contractual terms, geographical conditions, etc could differ between the two parties.
